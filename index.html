Welcome!

I am currently a second-year Master candidate at Nankai University, under the advisement of Prof. Jufeng Yang. I received my Bachelor degree from Ocean University of China in 2012. My research interests include Computer Vision and Machine Learning. Recently, I have learned a lot on researching sparse subspace clustering, generative adversarial networks, image classification and various low-level vision problems.

During my research, I feel very appreciate to cooperate with Prof. Ming-Ming Cheng (Nankai University), Prof. Ming-Hsuan Yang (UC Merced), and Prof. Paul L. Rosin (Cardiff University).
