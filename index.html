Welcome!

I am currently a third-year Master candidate at Nankai University, under the supervision of Prof. Jufeng Yang. I received my Bachelor degree from Ocean University of China in 2016. My research interests include Computer Vision and Machine Learning. Specifically, I am interested in sparse subspace clustering, generative adversarial networks, image classification and various low-level vision problems such as image enhancement.

During my research, I cooperated with Prof. Ming-Ming Cheng (Nankai University), Prof. Ming-Hsuan Yang (UC Merced), and Prof. Paul L. Rosin (Cardiff University).
