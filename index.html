Welcome!

I am currently a first-year Ph.D. student at The Hong Kong Polytechnic University (PolyU), under the supervision of Prof. Lei Zhang (IEEE Fellow). I received my Master degree at Nankai University in 2019, under the supervision of Prof. Jufeng Yang. I received my Bachelor degree from Ocean University of China in 2016. My research interests include Computer Vision and Machine Learning. Specifically, I am interested in sparse subspace clustering, generative adversarial networks, and various low-level vision problems such as image enhancement and image-to-image translation.

During my research, I cooperated with Prof. Ming-Ming Cheng (Nankai University), Prof. Ming-Hsuan Yang (UC Merced), and Prof. Paul L. Rosin (Cardiff University).
